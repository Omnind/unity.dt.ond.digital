{"Title":"Pivot Tool","Text":"<size=16><b>Pivot Tool</b></size>\r\n\r\nTo (re)position a pivot of a GameObjects, you will need to use the pivot tool. You can find this under <i>Utilities > Pivot Tool</i>. \r\n\r\nThis will open a new window. \r\nFor this tool, you will need <b>two</b> GameObjects. One GameObject (A) of which the pivot needs to change, one GameObject (B) to function as a reference for the new pivot. \r\n\r\n- Position (B) so that the its pivot is in the <b>exact position and/or rotation</b> of which you want the pivot of (A) to be. \r\n\r\n- Drag (B) into the <b>Align pivot to object</b> field of the Pivot Tool.\r\n\r\n- Select (A) in the hierarchy. \r\n\r\n- Determine whether you want to change the pivot position, rotation or both with <b>Pivot Movement Type</b>. Then click <b>Change Pivot</b> to apply the changes.\r","DocumentationURL":"https://unit040.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/PUD/pages/280953276/Pivot+Tool","SubComponents":"","Locked":true}
